OBJECTIVES

Developing an organizational framework to resolve grievances of the students and other stakeholders Enlightening the students on their duties and responsibilities to access benefits. Ensuring effective solution to the student's grievances Obtaining where possible a speedy resolution to the problem. Creating social awareness to female staff members and students in the institute about the relevant issues related to women. Encouraging them to participate in the activities which are meant for their development as women. Organizing guest lectures to enhance awareness of rights, safety, and health care. Arranging seminars, workshops for creating general awareness and orientation to students, teachers, non-teaching staff for self-employment schemes for the encouragement of self-reliance among women, and their active and sustained participation. Training programs for learning alertness and classes for training on interests among them. Provide a forum for discussion and interaction with eminent ladies.

Anti Ragging Committee

To oversee the anti-ragging measures being adopted in the campus and to advise on changes, if any, so as to make the measures effective to prevent ragging of the students, especially fresher?s. To take report from the anti-ragging squads and to monitor their effectiveness.

Discipline Committee

The college gives utmost priority for discipline and every one, student or staff, is bound to follow the rules and regulations of the college and maintain strict discipline The disciplinary committee consisting of discipline in charge and senior faculty in different departments maintains & enforces strict discipline in the college campus.

STUDENT
